Industry

Enterprise Strategic Intelligence AI Engine

An AI-powered platform that integrates multi-source talent, project, and market data to generate dynamic strategic insights. It supports due diligence, risk monitoring, and competitive benchmarking, helping investors and enterprises make evidence-based decisions with greater speed and accuracy.

  • Stack: LLMs + Agents, knowledge graphs, time-series modeling, causal inference, network analysis.

Flight Price Prediction Project

A machine learning system that leverages large-scale flight, booking, and pricing data to forecast fare movements and recommend optimal ticket purchase timing. By combining predictive modeling with adaptive decision strategies, it helps users and agencies maximize savings in highly volatile markets.

  • Stack: Transformer-based time-series models, reinforcement learning, attention mechanisms, multi-agent simulation.

3D Real-Scene Digital Cockpit Project

An immersive cockpit system that integrates 3D visualization with advanced data analytics to provide real-time monitoring of operational metrics. The platform enables early-warning alerts and intelligent diagnostics, supporting safer and more efficient decision-making in dynamic environments.

  • Stack: 3D rendering engines, real-time data streaming and processing, anomaly detection with deep learning, sensor fusion, predictive analytics.
